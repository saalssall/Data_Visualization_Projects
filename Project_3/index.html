Project 03

Food Centre Orders Analysis

Dataset — food_center.csv
Tools — Python, Pandas, Matplotlib
Type — Data Visualization

An analysis of food centre order data exploring which categories, cuisines, and centre types drive the most orders. Visualizations include bar charts and grouped comparisons across checkout price, base price, and order volume.

Key Findings
Top category by checkout price
Beverages were the most ordered category when ranked by checkout price — people spent more on beverages than any other food category.
Cuisine by centre type
Type A centres had the highest visitor count across all cuisines. Italian food at Type A centres recorded the highest order frequency of all combinations.
Top category by base price
Beverages also led when ranked by base price, confirming their dominance across both pricing metrics.
Top category by order volume
Rice Bowl had the highest number of orders by category, making it the most popular individual food item.
Top cuisine overall
Italian food recorded the highest total number of orders across all cuisines in the dataset.
