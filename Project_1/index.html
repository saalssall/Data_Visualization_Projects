Project 01

Gun Deaths in the United States

Exploratory analysis of gun-related deaths, focusing on trends, causes, and affected demographics.

Overview

Introduction

This project explores gun-related deaths in the United States using publicly available datasets. The goal is to identify patterns and understand the scale of the issue.

Key Findings

Overall Trend

Gun deaths remain a persistent public health issue across the years.

Main Causes

Most cases involve homicide, suicide, and accidental shootings.

Demographics

Different age groups and populations are affected in different ways.

Conclusion

Understanding these patterns can help guide prevention strategies, policy decisions, and public awareness efforts.
